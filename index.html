Flagship Research Project

Agnirodhak — Forest Fire Early Warning System for Uttarakhand

U-Net Satellite Data Spatiotemporal ML Google Earth Engine

Abstract

Agnirodhak is a machine-learning–driven forest fire early warning system designed to forecast next-day wildfire risk across Uttarakhand, India. The system integrates vegetation indices (NDVI), meteorological variables, terrain attributes, and historical fire occurrence data derived from satellite observations.

Methodology

A U-Net convolutional neural network performs pixel-wise risk segmentation on 1 km × 1 km spatial grids. The pipeline includes large-scale ETL processing (30M+ samples), normalization, temporal stacking, and tiled inference.

Results

The model achieves stable next-day forecasts with approximately 93% pixel-level accuracy on validation data. Predicted high-risk zones are visualized through an interactive web dashboard.
